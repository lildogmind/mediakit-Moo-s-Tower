# 🏠 Moo's Tower — Cozy Focus App

A cozy focus timer app where you help **Moo**, a cartoon orange cat, build a virtual tower by staying focused! Inspired by *Focus Friend by Hank Green*.

## How It Works

1. **Set a timer** — Pick 5 to 120 minutes of focus time
2. **Moo knits** — While you focus, Moo sits and knits in their cozy room
3. **Earn fish** 🐟 — Complete your session to earn 1 fish per minute
4. **Decorate** — Spend fish in the shop to furnish Moo's room with plants, furniture, lighting, and cozy items
5. **Build the tower** — Unlock new floors as you rack up focus minutes

## Features

- 🕐 **Flexible timer** with preset buttons (5–90 min) and custom slider
- 🐱 **Animated Moo** — idle bouncing, knitting during focus, happy jumps on completion
- 🛒 **30+ shop items** across 5 categories (Furniture, Plants, Decor, Cozy, Lighting)
- 🗼 **8-floor tower** to unlock through cumulative focus time
- 📊 **Stats dashboard** with weekly chart, streaks, and lifetime stats
- 🌙 **Day/night window** that matches your real time
- 💾 **Auto-saves** to localStorage — your progress persists
- ☕ **Break timer** with Pomodoro-style 5-minute breaks
- ✨ **Floating particles** and cozy warm aesthetic

## Tech

Pure HTML + CSS + JavaScript. No frameworks, no build tools, no dependencies. Just open `index.html` in a browser.

## Deploy

Works great on GitHub Pages — just enable Pages on the `main` branch.
